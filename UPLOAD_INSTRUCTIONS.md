# Upload Instructions

This repository structure is ready. You need to manually upload your project files into the correct folders.

## What to Upload and Where

### notebooks/
Upload your 6 Jupyter notebook PDF files here.
- Go to the `notebooks/` folder
- Click "Add file" then "Upload files"
- Drag and drop all notebook PDFs
- Click "Commit changes"

### report/
Upload your final report here.
- File to upload: `Cybercrime_Pavan.docx`
- Go to the `report/` folder
- Click "Add file" then "Upload files"

### presentation/
Upload your presentation here.
- File to upload: your `.pptx` file
- Go to the `presentation/` folder
- Click "Add file" then "Upload files"

### data/
Upload your compiled dataset here.
- File to upload: your `.csv` dataset file
- Go to the `data/` folder
- Click "Add file" then "Upload files"

## How to Upload a File to a Specific Folder

1. Navigate to the folder in the repo (e.g. click on `notebooks/`)
2. Click the "Add file" button (top right)
3. Select "Upload files"
4. Drag and drop your file(s) into the box
5. Scroll down and click "Commit changes"

Repeat this for each folder.
